The model

Simple to start, flexible to scale

No lengthy onboarding. No rigid contracts. Built to move at your hiring pace.

01
Alignment call
A 20-minute conversation about your hiring needs, team structure, timelines and what success looks like for you.
02
Retainer agreement
A simple engagement letter covering scope, hours, rate and reporting cadence. No bureaucracy, no lengthy procurement.
03
I get to work
Onboard to your ATS, meet the HR team, understand your JDs — and start delivering within the first week.
04
Ongoing delivery
Weekly hiring updates, live pipeline reports and regular HR team syncs. You always know where things stand.
05
Flexible scaling
Ramp up when hiring is heavy, pull back when it's quiet. The retainer adjusts to your actual demand — no wasted spend.
06
HR-only interface
I work exclusively with your HR team — not business-facing. This keeps governance clean and your HR function in full control.
